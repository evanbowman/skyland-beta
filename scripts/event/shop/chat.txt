On the surface, goblins tend to live underground. This protects them from some of the radiation!
Goblins don't usually eat each other, but they will do it if hungry enough.
Drones can be destroyed by dynamite!
If you have a radar installed, you can even use transporters to warp goblins from their ship to yours. I wonder why someone would do that?
When you select 'continue building' after a fight, you can move blocks around for free! I wonder what that means...
Repair drones can put out fires!
Fires can spread through portals! Be careful when building portal networks!
